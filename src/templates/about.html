HCIKE Foundation is a non-governmental organization dedicated to transforming lives by delivering essential health services to those who need them most. Founded by a team of passionate healthcare professionals and social advocates, we work tirelessly to ensure that every person, regardless of their economic status, has access to the healthcare they deserve

Our vision, our misson, our story, our core values

Our story

The HCIKE Foundation was established in memory of Chief Hyacinth Chukwunenye Ike (popularly known as 'Ochiri Ozuo'), a philanthropist renowned for his unwavering support of less privileged individuals through scholarships, community projects, and infrastructural development. Chief HC Ike's legacy of compassion and community service continues to inspire our work.
