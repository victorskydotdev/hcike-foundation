The man, Chief Hyacinth Chukwunyere Ike

CHIEF HYACINTH IKE a philanthropist to the core, was born in April 9th 1936 in a small village Umuokiri Ehime Mbano in Imo State but lived in Owerri metropolitan city where he ran his private construction company until he died in 2015. His philanthropic deposition was not felt in Owerri but also in his community. He provided scholarship scheme to many less privileged in his community. He was popularly called Chief HC IKE and freely used his God’s given blessings to bless others in his community and beyond.

The foundation is in memory of his support, help and philanthropy to the less privileged in the society. Help us keep him alive by keeping his selfless works to humanity alive.
